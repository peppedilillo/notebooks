def pandas_from(fits):
    fits_path = Path(fits)

    with fitsio.open(fits_path) as fits_file:
        # would be nice to just pd.DataFrame(fits_file[1].data) but fits files are strictly big endian
        df = pd.DataFrame(np.array(fits_file[1].data).byteswap().newbyteorder())
    return df

qi2k = (lambda n: chr(65 + n))

Conclusions¶

  1. Low energy peaks events have multiplicity greater than one. Most of these events are from channels sharing a scintillator.
  2. For low energy peaks no particular correlation with the amplitude of preceding events was observed.
